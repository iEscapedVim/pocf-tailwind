# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 16th December 2025

Pakistan Orphan Care Forum ("**POCF**", "**we**", "**our**", or "**us**") operates the website **POCF (Pakistan Orphan Care Forum)** and a related Facebook-connected application used solely to fetch and display public Facebook posts from our official Facebook Page on our website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how information is collected, used, and safeguarded.

---

## 1. About POCF

Pakistan Orphan Care Forum (POCF) is a national platform working to improve the lives of orphans across Pakistan.

* We have supported **101,336 orphans** across **166 districts**.
* **44 orphanages** are operated by member organizations.
* **6,837 orphans** have reached college and university education.
* **20 reputable INGOs/NGOs** are part of the POCF network.

Our work focuses on:

* **Education** – empowering orphans through quality education
* **Health** – ensuring physical and mental well-being
* **Care & Support** – providing family and orphan care assistance
* **Orphanages** – offering safe accommodation, healthcare, and development opportunities

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

* IP address
* Browser type and version
* Device type
* Pages visited and time spent on the website

This information is used **only for website performance, security, and analytics**.

### b) Facebook Data

Our website uses a Facebook-connected app to **fetch and display public posts** from POCF’s official Facebook Page.

* We **do not collect** personal data of Facebook users
* We **do not store** Facebook user profiles, comments, messages, or private information
* Only **publicly available page content** (posts, images, captions) is displayed

The app complies fully with **Meta (Facebook) Platform Policies**.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Display public Facebook posts on our website
* Improve website performance and user experience
* Ensure website security and prevent misuse
* Analyze general website traffic (non-identifiable)

We **do not**:

* Sell personal data
* Share personal data with advertisers
* Use data for marketing without consent

---

## 4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to:

* Maintain basic website functionality
* Analyze anonymous usage patterns

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share personal data with third parties except:

* When required by law
* To comply with legal obligations
* To protect the rights, safety, and security of POCF

Facebook content is governed by **Facebook’s own Privacy Policy**, and users should review Meta’s policies separately.

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information against:

* Unauthorized access
* Alteration
* Disclosure
* Destruction

While no online system is 100% secure, we strive to follow best practices to safeguard data.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

POCF’s website is intended for general informational purposes.

* We do **not knowingly collect personal data from children**
* If you believe a child’s personal information has been shared unintentionally, please contact us immediately

No Facebook user data is stored beyond temporary caching required for displaying posts.
